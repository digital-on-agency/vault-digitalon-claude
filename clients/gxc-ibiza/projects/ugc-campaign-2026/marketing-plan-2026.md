## 4. Launch Strategy — Opening Season

### Pre-Launch (Marzo-Aprile)

**Settimana 1-2 Marzo: Teaser Phase**
- Video misterioso: mani che impastano, suono di piatti, luci che si accendono
- Copy: "Ibiza has never tasted anything like this."
- Waitlist landing page con lead magnet (menu preview PDF)

**Settimana 3-4 Marzo: Reveal Phase**
- Chef reveal, venue sneak peek
- Waitlist target: 2.000 email entro fine marzo

**Settimana 1-2 Aprile: Build Phase**
- Early bird launch (20% off, solo waitlist, 48h window)
- Influencer private tasting event (15-20 creator)
- Hotel concierge kit distribution

**Settimana 3-4 Aprile: Hype Phase**
- Embargo lift: influencer content goes live simultaneously
- PR push: articoli su Timeout, Condé Nast Traveller
- Ads full funnel launch
- Countdown to opening: daily Stories

### Launch Week (1-7 Maggio)

| Giorno | Azione |
|--------|--------|
| Lun 1 | Final countdown content, team prep BTS |
| Mar 2 | VIP/Press preview night (50 ospiti selezionati) |
| Mer 3 | Embargo lift press night → reviews published |
| Gio 4 | OPENING NIGHT — Live IG, photographer, videographer |
| Ven 5 | Morning-after recap, UGC reshare, first reviews |
| Sab 6 | Weekend show — peak capacity target |
| Dom 7 | "Week 1 Wrap" aftermovie, guest reaction compilation |
